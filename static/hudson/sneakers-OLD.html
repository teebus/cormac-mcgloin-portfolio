    Calisthenics isn’t just a sport. It’s a way of life. It challenges the limits of the human body with a relentless energy at its core. Much like practitioners Koda and Shorai, Hudson Sneakers are defined by their movement. Designed by Victoria Haddon and Rachel Alsbury, two iconic styles are born into Hudson’s first ever Sneaker collection.

    Your Sneakers. Your Story.

    Hudson sneakers let your movement do the talking. An entirely unique sole means there is nothing quite like them on the planet, meaning they won’t stop until you do.

    Minimalist style. Maximum comfort.

    Rendered with one single piece of premium leather, Sime defies expectations with a contrasting internal neoprene sock that allows outstanding movement without compromising on style.

    Control.
    Defined and redefined.

    With an exposed neoprene and nubuck upper, the multi-laced Brodie offers supreme versatility for maximum control. It’s technical, multi-laced silhouette sits on a speckled outsole for a slick finish that stands out without shouting.

    Express your identity.

    A multitude of colourways allows you to exude your personal style, whether you’re using them for training or for day-to-day adventures.
